A quiet reflection each day.

Once Said is a calm daily reflection app for iPhone. Each morning it shows you one quote from a historical thinker, with one important word hidden until you are ready to reveal it.

There are no streaks, no scores, no badges, no completion screens, and no celebratory copy. The app does not try to be your morning coach. It sits on the home screen like a slim, well-printed book left on a bedside table.

Once Said is offline-first. The full library ships with the app. There is no account, no subscription, no analytics, and no tracking. You pay once and the app is yours.
