A quiet reflection each day.

Once Said is a calm daily reflection app for iPhone. It presents one quotation from a historical thinker, with one key word hidden until you choose to reveal it.

There are no streaks, scores, badges or celebratory prompts. It is designed to feel simple and unobtrusive, more like a small book on the bedside table than a productivity tool.

The full quote library is included in the app and works offline. There is no account, no subscription and no tracking. It is a one-time purchase.
