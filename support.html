Support

A short collection of answers to common questions. If your question is not here, please email support@oncesaid.app.

How do I change the daily reminder time?

Open Settings, tap Daily reminder, then tap the time row to pick a new time. The reminder follows your phone's local timezone and adjusts automatically across daylight saving changes. If you turn the reminder off and back on, your previously chosen time is remembered.

Can I see past quotes I have read?

Quotes you have saved by tapping the heart appear in the Favourites screen, on the Saved tab. The Recently seen tab shows reflections you have revealed in the last few sessions but have not saved. Beyond that, past quotes do not stay in a history view. Once Said is designed around the single daily moment rather than a back-catalogue.

Can I turn off the hidden-word interaction and just see the full quote?

Yes. Open Settings, tap Reveal mode, and choose Always revealed. The quote will appear fully on every daily screen from then on. You can switch back to Hidden by default at any time.

Does the app need an internet connection?

No. Every quote, every screen, every font, and every other asset ships inside the app. Once Said works in aeroplane mode and never makes a network request during normal use.

Does the app collect any data about me?

No. There are no analytics, no third-party trackers, no remote API calls, and no account system. Your category preferences, saved reflections, reminder settings, and reading history live only on your device. The Privacy Notice covers this in full.

I noticed a typo or a misattribution in a quote. How do I report it?

Please email support@oncesaid.app with the quote text and the issue. Quotes in the bundled library are public-domain or otherwise approved for inclusion, and the curation pass before release reviewed every line, but reports help us catch anything that slipped through. Corrections ship in app updates.

Contact

For all questions about the app, the dataset, accessibility, or anything else, please email support@oncesaid.app.
