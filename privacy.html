Privacy Notice

Once Said is designed to collect as little personal data as possible.

1. Information stored on your device

The app may store information locally on your device, such as:

  • selected categories
  • reveal mode
  • reminder settings
  • saved reflections
  • recently seen reflections
  • shown-history
  • acceptance of Terms of Use and Privacy Notice

This information is used to make the app work and personalise your experience.

2. Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses your device's local notification system to schedule a reminder.

You can disable reminders in the app or through your device settings.

3. Analytics and tracking

Once Said does not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or cross-app tracking in v1.

If analytics are added in a future version, this Privacy Notice should be updated before release.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your saved reflections, recently seen reflections, local shown-history, or app preferences with advertisers.

5. App Store data

If you download or purchase the app through the Apple App Store, Apple may process information relating to your download, purchase, subscription, device, and account under Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

6. Support emails

If you contact us for support, we will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message, screenshots, device details, or any other details you include.

We use this information only to respond to your enquiry, manage support, and keep appropriate records.

7. Data retention

Local app data remains on your device unless you delete it, reset the app, delete the app, or your device removes it.

Support emails may be retained for a reasonable period to manage support and records.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to certain uses of your personal data.

To exercise your rights, contact us at:

support@oncesaid.app

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact:

support@oncesaid.app
